üîë The Key Enabler: Self-Efficacy as Prescriptive Training

Before any of this works, employees need the self-efficacy cascade (Levels 1-4) taught prescriptively: Industry context ‚Üí Company mission ‚Üí Org structure ‚Üí Team responsibilities. This foundation allows employees to identify their own gaps and articulate learning needs. Then managers apply this framework to categorize those needs appropriately. Without this prescriptive foundation, employees can't effectively engage with reactive resources or drive proactive learning.
